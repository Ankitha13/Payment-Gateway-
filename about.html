About Us

This is an Indian non-profit and non-governmental organization that provides care and protection to the homeless and those in need. We provide residential care and healthcare support. In 1997 online giving was a novelty. Few companies existed to help non-profits raise money online.We set out to change that by creating a company founded on the desire to blend a business opportunity with a chance to do some good. We endeavour to act according to the following principles. Peace,Life and Love Delight the Customer Honesty and Openness.
